Yo on its head

Introducing YOMOTE, the app that lets you Yo for anything.

When you Yo one of the Usernames on YOMOTE something happens. Simply search through the created usernames and when you find one you like, add them to your Yo contacts.

For Developers: we make creating Yo apps fast and easy. Write a few lines of code (on top of our available pre-defined library) and tell us what information you need to know from your users to make the app work. We provide you a callback endpoint on our server. Simple. This will save you the time of writing an whole webapp just to use the Yo API, the cost of hosting the app on your own servers, and the hassle of associating information with individual users. All of this is taken care of by us, so you don't need to worry.
